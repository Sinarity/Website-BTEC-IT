Hello, and welcome to this website about endangered species and how we can help them. Why do we need to save endangered animals? Because many ecosystems rely on them in order to survive. Many ecosystems that we have are slowly being torn apart and destroyed by us humans.

This website will show you certain examples of endangered animals, and how humans have caused them to become endangered. This website will also show you the charities that make sure these animals are protected. This website will also contain a quiz that will ask you twenty questions about various different topics relating to the subject of endangered animals.
